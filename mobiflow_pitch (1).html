The Solution

WorkPods. Your commute,
on autopilot.

MobiFlow's AI engine identifies colleagues and neighbors with identical commute routes and auto-groups them into recurring ride-shares. Once matched, your WorkPod handles the rest — forever.

01
AI Matches Your WorkPod

Enter your home pin code, office address, and typical departure window. MobiFlow's matching engine (updated nightly) finds 3–5 verified colleagues or nearby professionals on your exact route. You approve the group once.

02
Calendar-Synced Scheduling

Connect your Google or Outlook calendar. MobiFlow auto-schedules pickups around your meetings. WFH day? The app notifies your pod automatically. No daily booking. No group chats about pickup times.

03
Verified, Safe, Auto-Billed

All members Aadhaar-verified. Drivers background-checked and rated. Live tracking shared with emergency contacts. UPI auto-split billing happens in the background. Fare fixed for 30 days — no surge, ever.

04
Your Pod Becomes Your Team

68% of beta users say their WorkPod became one of their closest professional relationships. The commute stops being dead time and becomes the 30-minute daily sync that remote work killed.
